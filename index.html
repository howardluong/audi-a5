The Outer Aspects

The Audi A5 Coupé is the modern interpretation of the classic coupé. A sculpted hood, short overhangs, and flowing roofline are complemented by the strong shoulder lines and prominent Singleframe grille. Inspired by the past, but built for the future.

The future in line design.

Starting with the large, wide Singleframe grille and the focused stare of the headlines, lines flow along the body to craft the beautiful shape of the A5 Coupé.

×

Mesmerizing Wheels.

18-inch wheels come standard with the A5 Coupé. Available in three designs, they perfectly complement the sharp looks. Optional 19-inch wheels further enhance the sporty style of the Coupé.

Embrace the sharper look.

For those who relish in design, the optional S Line Sport package features meticulously crafted elements, including 19-inch wheels, distinctive exterior styling, front sport seats, and a flat-bottom steering wheel.

×

The Inner Beauty and Technology

The inner beauty.

Luxury from the inside out. With comfort, style, and convenience at the core of every detail, the A5 Coupé turns every drive into an experience. Plus, you can customize your A5 Coupé with leather colour and inlay options to deliver on your unique standards of luxury. This stunning red interior features the highest quality fine Nappa leather, black headliner, illuminated door sills, and stainless steel pedals. (Not available with S Line Sport Package.)

×

Everything at your fingertips.

A smart-looking interior should feature intelligent technology. The sleek and subtle MMI Touch uses handwriting recognition so you can input your destination with more comfort and speed.

Intelligent. Innovative. Integrated.

Assistance and security are at the core of Audi connect. Take advantage of fully integrated and seamless assistance tools, including intelligent navigation, remote vehicle services, car finder, and roadside assistance. Experience peace-of-mind with security features, including emergency call and stolen vehicle locator. (Optional add-on feature.)

Keep your personal space.

Convenience and safety are two pillars of Audi driver assistance. The adaptive cruise control with Stop&Go not only adapts your speed to maintain a safe distance from the car in front, it can also slow you to a complete stop and resume the pre-set speed once traffic moves. (Optional add-on feature.)

Navigate any traffic.

Navigate traffic in your A5 Coupé with ease. The Audi traffic jam assistant uses ultrasonic sensors to combine acceleration, braking, and steering guidance at speeds under 60 km/h in congested traffic. The feature identifies lanes, vehicles, and structures to calculate a safe vehicle path, and even maintains the distance to the vehicle ahead. (Optional add-on feature.)
